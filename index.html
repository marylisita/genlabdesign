A Research Lab for Creative Technology

GenLab is an independent digital laboratory and editorial platform dedicated to the critical analysis of generative algorithms, creative coding, and modern digital aesthetics. I bridge the gap between abstract computer science concepts and human-centric design, providing deep dives into how machine logic shapes our online and physical environments.

From exploring the mathematics of recursive structures and shader programming to analyzing the architectural implications of autonomous AI agents, my mission is to empower developers, artists, and researchers with high-quality, human-authored insights into the future of technology.
