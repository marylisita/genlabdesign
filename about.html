System.Author

Blending code, art, and the digital world.

I am a creative coder and digital artist from Brazil. My interest in the digital world started early, driven by video games, computers, and the messy possibilities of the internet.

In 2021, I joined UFRJ (Federal University of Rio de Janeiro), which gave me room to explore academic research, algorithmic aesthetics, and digital fabrication. Instead of just consuming technology, I wanted to understand how to build it, break it, and use it as a medium for artistic expression.

I currently work at Metaverse Agency, EBAT (Escola Brasileira de Arte e Tecnologia), and do research at LAID (Laboratório Aberto de Inovação e Design) where we look into spatial computing, education, and digital innovation. Before this, I worked at the NANO Lab and completed a design internship at ECOA PUC-Rio. This background shows me exactly how design, art, and programming come together in practice.
